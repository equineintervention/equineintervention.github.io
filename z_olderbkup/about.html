Equine Intervention's Mission

The mission of Equine Intervention, LLC is to provide all clients the best experience in equine assisted psychotherapy (EAP) and equine assisted learning (EAL) available. This service shall not be restricted to the client's ability to pay for services. It is our duty to protect the privacy and emotional safety of all clients at all times. We will adhere to all ethical protocol of the EAGALA model and mental health standards. The Equine Intervention facilitating team will always act in the best interest of the client without compromising the safety of our horses or our treatment team.
