EAP Program for Troops & their Families

Troops & their Families: Our programs for soldiers and their families are divided into three phases based on the current status of their military service. Each series focuses on the particular obstacles the individual or family is facing including, but not limited to: Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), anxiety, depression, addictions, adjustment disorders, and family conflict.
