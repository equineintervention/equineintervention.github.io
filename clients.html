Coming to EAP On Your Own:

If you are interested in experiencing the benefits of therapeutic service through EAP you may contact us by phone, email, or our contact link on the homepage. We offer individual, family, group, and couples counseling Monday through Friday by appointment only. Equine Intervention is enrolled with most major insurance companies and utilize a billing service for filing claims. In the event that you are not satisfied with the equine-assisted format we also partner with Palmetto Counseling of Hartsville to provide traditional office sessions.
