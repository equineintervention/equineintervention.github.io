Horses and Humans Scholarship Program

Equine Intervention, LLC partners with Palmetto Counseling of Hartsville as well as the South Carolina Awareness and Rescue of Equines Organization (S.C.A.R.E.) to offer several specialized programs throughout the year. We apply regularly for grant funding to assist with programs for at-risk youth, children in placement, military families, child and adult survivors of trauma, and educational programs designed to assist with parenting challenges, life changes, stress management and community wellness. Our partnership with S.C.A.R.E. is unique in that as we receive funds for these programs we will be able to foster and rehabilitate horses rescued from their own traumatic experiences. These horses will be an integral part our existing equine team and provide a unique opportunity for humans and horses to heal together. Anyone wishing to assist with our programs may donate directly to S.C.A.R.E. (a nonprofit 501(c)3 organization), however you must specify that your donation is for the Horses and Humans Program provided by Equine Intervention, LLC and S.C.A.R.E. Please feel free to contact us for more information by phone or email.
