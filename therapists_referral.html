Referring Clients to Equine Intervention

If you would like to refer clients to Equine Intervention, LLC, we have a facilitating team available and are able to bill insurance for most insurance plans. Our team members are all Licensed Professional Counselors and most are certified by EAGALA. Our professional team boasts over 25 years of experience in education, mental health practice, and numerous specialized treatment modalities. Treatment varies widely from client to client, but on average clients attend 12-15 sessions. Referring clients to our treatment team will allow your clients to benefit from EAP even if you do not have time to participate as part of the treatment team. Observations and assessment can be provided with client consent to the referring therapist if you are or will continue to treat outside of EAP.
